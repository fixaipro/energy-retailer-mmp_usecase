The Solution

A full-stack MMP deployment — from technical integration to business intelligence — built for a privacy-first world.

Kochava MMP · Global

One platform.
Global scale.
Full visibility.

Kochava was selected as Energy-provider's global Mobile Measurement Partner and deployed across all media platforms worldwide. The brief was clear: improve user experience, monetise Loyalty programme data, and grow the user base — while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and consent compliance.

The rollout quickly grew and expanded Energy-provider's user base at a cheaper cost than traditional channels, while providing business-critical insights that traditional media measurement simply could not deliver.

  • 📊

    Lifetime Value (LTV) Modelling

    Predictive LTV models built on Kochava's enriched first-party data, enabling Energy-provider to identify, target and retain its most commercially valuable users with precision.

  • 🔮

    Predictive Churn Analysis

    Early-warning churn signals identified users at risk of disengaging, enabling proactive retention campaigns before loyalty points expired and app uninstalls occurred.

  • 📱

    SKAN 4.0 · iOS Privacy Compliance

    Full SKAdNetwork deployment ensured iOS attribution remained accurate and actionable post-ATT, driving the 71% iOS revenue growth whilst remaining fully compliant with Apple's privacy framework.

  • 💹

    ROAS Measurement & Optimisation

    Return on Ad Spend was measured at the campaign, channel and creative level — enabling budget reallocation in real-time to highest-performing acquisition sources.

  • 🔁

    Retention & Funnel Analytics

    Deep funnel analysis revealed the install-to-registration drop-off. Targeted interventions in the onboarding flow reduced this gap by 50%, directly expanding the registered user base.

  • 🌍

    Global Media Platform Integration

    Kochava was integrated across all media buying platforms globally — Google, Meta, Apple Search Ads and programmatic — creating a single, unified view of cross-channel performance for the first time.
